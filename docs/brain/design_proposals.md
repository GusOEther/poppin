# Filter UI Design Proposals

Here are three concepts for integrating Date and Category filters into the Bubble View.

````carousel
![Option 1: Floating Glass Pills](/home/mark/.gemini/antigravity/brain/78151308-7b67-4b4b-aa52-6beea5d8542e/filter_design_floating_pills_1767122509560.png)
### Option 1: Floating Glass Pills
- **Concept**: Minimal interference with the bubbles.
- **Location**: Floating just below the header title.
- **Interaction**: Horizontally scrollable lists.
- **Pros**: Very modern, keeps focus on the bubbles.

<!-- slide -->

![Option 2: Bottom Action Bar](/home/mark/.gemini/antigravity/brain/78151308-7b67-4b4b-aa52-6beea5d8542e/filter_design_bottom_bar_1767122525810.png)
### Option 2: Bottom Control Bar
- **Concept**: Separates controls from the content entirely.
- **Location**: Fixed glass bar above the bottom navigation.
- **Pros**: Easy thumb reach, clean separation.
- **Cons**: Takes up screen real estate from the bubbles.

<!-- slide -->

![Option 3: Expanded Header](/home/mark/.gemini/antigravity/brain/78151308-7b67-4b4b-aa52-6beea5d8542e/filter_design_expanded_header_1767122542228.png)
### Option 3: Expanded Header Area
- **Concept**: Integrates filters directly into the "What's poppin'" section.
- **Location**: Static section at the top.
- **Pros**: Clear hierarchy, very organised.
- **Cons**: Pushes the bubbles further down the screen.
````

**What do you think?**
- **Option 1** fits our "Neon Nights" aesthetic best (floating, glass).
- **Option 2** is very functional but might feel "heavy".
- **Option 3** is classic but perhaps less "playful".

Which direction should we go?
